The mechanical meanings below are based on isolated listening. They remain
inferred until their timing is compared with the original in-game mix.

Current prototype mapping:

- start: `event_0220`;
- insert: `event_0216` + `event_0241` variants;
- no reload finish.

Gameplay testing found that the previous final cue belongs to the bolt cycle
after a shot, as with the W-870. The runtime schedules `event_0224`
approximately one second after a shot only when ammunition remains. If the
shot emptied the magazine, the bolt cycle is suppressed and deferred until
the following reload ends.

## Preliminary implementation candidates

### Speaker

- Reload layer: `event_0216` followed by `event_0241`, after gameplay timing
  confirms whether they are two separate phases.
- Bolt action: `event_0220`.
- Chamber accent: `event_0224`.
- Optional casing detail: use `event_0226`, `event_0228`, or `event_0230`;
  validate whether `event_0237` belongs to the same family.
- Material-routed casing detail: `surface_0234`.
- Scope adjustment: `event_0254`.
- Optional close mechanical details: `event_0222`, `event_0254`, and
  `event_0256`.

### Haptics

- Highest-priority mechanical layer: `event_0220` (9/10).
- Strong reload lock: `event_0241` (8/10).
- Possible metal-impact candidate: `event_0237` (8/10), pending exact source.
- Magazine insertion: `event_0216` (7/10).
- Chamber/feed motion: `event_0224` (6/10).

`event_0239` has the highest raw speaker and haptics scores, but its earlier
`Fire` classification was based only on waveform character and is considered
incorrect for this candidate set. Do not implement it until its actual
mechanical event is identified.
