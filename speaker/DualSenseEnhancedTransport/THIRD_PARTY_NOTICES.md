# Third-Party Build Inputs

The experimental binaries were built locally from:

- [WujekFoliarz/duaLib](https://github.com/WujekFoliarz/duaLib), commit
  `03bad1bea2a36561b520846776f2a07ced6773e0`, MIT license.
- [libusb/hidapi](https://github.com/libusb/hidapi), commit
  `8c9cbf6c020974d23e4690497778f3df173d1166`, available under the repository's
  documented HIDAPI licensing options.
- [mstorsjo/llvm-mingw](https://github.com/mstorsjo/llvm-mingw), release
  `20260616`, used as the portable x64 compiler toolchain.

The original source archives and license files remain under `third_party/`.
