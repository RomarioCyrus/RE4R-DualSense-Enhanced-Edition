DualSense Enhanced Edition for RE4R
Third-Party Licenses
====================================================

This mod bundles the following open-source components: sound extraction
tools (ree-pak-cli, vgmstream), the native controller transport libraries
(duaLib, hidapi), and the audio bridge runtime (NAudio).
No Capcom audio assets are included or distributed.

The package includes original healing cues generated with AI tools by the mod
author, plus original synthesized haptic tones and haptic derivatives of those
author-owned cues. These are mod assets, not Capcom or Sony audio assets.
